Benefits
Maintainability: Centralizing resources like colors and images makes updates straightforward. Changing a single value or path reflects everywhere it's used.
Readability: Your widget files remain clean and focused on layout and functionality rather than low-level resource details.
Consistency: Prevents accidental usage of different shades or paths for the same resource.
Scalability: As your app grows, managing assets becomes easier with centralized files.
